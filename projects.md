### Trucey — AI Negotiation Coach

An AI-powered coaching tool designed to help users prepare for workplace negotiations. Grounded in principled negotiation theory, Trucey guides users through scenario-based practice with a conversational AI. The basis of a 267-person study examining how AI coaching affects self-efficacy, cognitive load, and over-reliance — with findings that complicate the straightforward case for AI coaching.

**Status:** Research prototype · onCare Lab, UIUC

### Coworking Illini — Study Buddy Matching App

Led front-end development and UX validation for a study buddy matching platform for UIUC students. Conducted 3 rounds of usability testing using think-aloud protocols and task analysis across diverse user groups, achieving 80% user satisfaction. Built for CS465: User Interface Design.

**Stack:** React · **Status:** Course project

### (Dis)Passion of Deduction

Used Named Entity Recognition and sentiment analysis across 9 detective novels to investigate the stereotype of the cold, rational detective in 19th-century crime fiction. A digital humanities project at the intersection of NLP and literary analysis. Built for CS2109/ENG2350: Introduction to Digital Humanities at Ashoka University.

**Stack:** Python, NLP · [GitHub](https://github.com)
